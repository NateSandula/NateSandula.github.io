Pinckney Boys Cross Country Heads to
Willow Metropark for SEC Jamboree #1

9/09/2024

Prodigy Nate Sandula Looking to Claim His Crown

The Pinckney Boys Cross Country team is heading to Willow Metropark to compete in the first SEC Jamboree meet on Tuesday. Headed by Zach Newman, Cole McCraw, and Kyle Osborne, Pinckney's cross country team is looking fast, with a strong 4th and 5th man of Nate Sandula and Gabe Maciag. They are competing against Adrian, Saline, Ann Arbor Pioneer, Tecumseh, Chelsea, Ann Arbor Huron, Ann Arbor Skyline, Dexter, Jackson, Monroe, Temperance Bedford, Ypsilanti Community, and Ypsilanti Lincoln.

In a interview with Nate Sandula, who has ran a 18:20 5k, he said he was very excited for this meet. Willow Metro Park is a fast and flat course meaning there will be some fast times this tuesday. I am very excited for this meet... I think our entire team is, said Nate Sandula, It is our chance to show everyone that we still got what it takes to be the best in the state. I know everyone was thinking that we were going to fall off after we lost Evan Loughridge, Paul Moore, and Ethan Sandula, but we are here today to show them that we are not done being the best.

As far as top runners go, Pinckney never seems to fail, with their top 2 runners, Zach Newman and Cole McCraw both running a 16:30 5k. Keep your eyes out for Adrian's top runner Moises Salazar who has ran a 15:47 5k.
