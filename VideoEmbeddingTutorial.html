How to Embed a Youtube Video into a Webpage

  • Step 1

    Find the video that you want to embed from Youtube, for example, this video about the nutritional value of meats

  • Step 2

    Click the share button underneath the video player

  • Step 3

    Click the embed button that pops up

  • Step 4

    Click the copy button next to the text

  • Step 5

    Press Ctrl + V in the body of your html code to paste the code
